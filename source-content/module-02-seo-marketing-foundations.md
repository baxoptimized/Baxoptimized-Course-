## 🎯 What SEO actually is

> 🖼️ **DIAGRAM PLACEHOLDER:** Three connected circles — Find, Understand, Trust — each with example tasks listed inside.

SEO = making it easy for Google to **Find** every page, **Understand** what each page is about, and **Trust** the site as credible.

| Job | Example tasks |
|---|---|
| **Find** | Sitemaps, internal linking, robots.txt |
| **Understand** | Titles, headings, body content |
| **Trust** | Reviews, citations, backlinks, site age |

> 💡 Every SEO task you'll ever do maps to one of these three words.

---

## 📍 How Google ranks local businesses

> 🖼️ **DIAGRAM PLACEHOLDER:** A balance scale — Relevance, Distance, Prominence — with Distance greyed out ("can't change this") and the other two highlighted ("you control these").

| Factor | Driven by | Can you influence it? |
|---|---|---|
| **Relevance** | GBP category, website content | ✅ Yes |
| **Distance** | Searcher's location vs business | ❌ No |
| **Prominence** | Reviews, citations, backlinks | ✅ Yes |

> 🎯 You can't change Distance. All your effort goes into Relevance and Prominence.

## 📝 On-page SEO — the real checklist

> 🖼️ **SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER:** An annotated Google search result, arrows pointing to exactly which text is the title tag vs. the meta description.

| Element | The rule | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Title tag** | Unique, under 60 chars, keyword + location | "Custom Home Builders Wollongong \| Smith Building Co" |
| **Meta description** | Unique, under 155 chars, gives a reason to click | "Award-winning builders in Wollongong. Free quotes." |
| **H1** | One per page, includes the keyword | "Custom Home Builders in Wollongong" |
| **Alt text** | Descriptive | ✅ "Kitchen renovation Thirroul" ❌ "IMG_4392" |
| **URLs** | Clean | `/services/kitchen-renovations` not `/page?id=42` |
| **Sitemap.xml** | Lists every page | Covered in Module 13 |
| **Page speed** | 90+ on PageSpeed | Covered in Module 12 |

## ⭐ Reviews, citations & the sacred rule

> 🔒 **NAP = Name, Address, Phone number.** Must be IDENTICAL everywhere online, word for word. Inconsistency confuses Google into thinking these are different businesses.

| Listing | Business name used |
|---|---|
| Own website | Smith Building Co Pty Ltd |
| Yellow Pages | Smith Building ❌ |
| TrueLocal | Smith Building Co ❌ |

> All three should read **exactly the same.** Pick one version, use it everywhere.

## 💰 What makes a site convert

- ✅ **Above-the-fold clarity** — within 2 seconds, know what/who/what-next
- ✅ **Clickable phone numbers** — `tel:` links, especially mobile
- ✅ **Multiple CTAs** — phone, form, sometimes booking
- ✅ **Trust signals** — real photos, real testimonials, ABN
- ✅ **Speed** — slow sites lose visitors before they even see the design
- ✅ **Mobile experience** — most local-trade traffic is mobile

> 📐 This checklist underlies every design (Module 5) and build (Module 6) decision from here.

## ⏳ Realistic timelines

> ⏳ **Realistic SEO timeline: 3-6 months minimum** for meaningful movement. New content can take 4-8 weeks just to be indexed.

| ✅ Use | ❌ Avoid |
|---|---|
| "We're aiming for..." | "Guaranteed first page" |
| "Typically, in 3-6 months..." | "Ranking in two weeks" |

> 🎯 Anyone promising fast SEO is lying or doesn't understand the system. Set the timeline upfront — when results land on schedule, you look like you knew what you were doing. Because you did.
