## 🧩 How every lesson works

> 🖼️ **DIAGRAM PLACEHOLDER:** Four icons in a row — 📄 Read, 👀 Look, 🤖 Prompt, ✅ Prove — matching the table below.

| | What it means |
|---|---|
| 📄 **Read** | A short burst of text — a few lines, not a page |
| 👀 **Look** | A screenshot, GIF, or short clip showing the real thing |
| 🤖 **Prompt** | An exact, ready-to-use AI prompt for that step |
| ✅ **Prove** | A quiz or a real checkpoint — something you actually submit |

**The rule throughout this course: short chunks, stacked.** If something feels like it's turning into a wall of text, that's a sign to stop reading and look at the example instead.

## 🔒 The locked-module system

> 💡 **Quizzes need 80% to pass. Unlimited retakes. You can't skip ahead** — the next module unlocks only once you've cleared the current one.

Why so strict? Because at the end of this, you're either working with real clients or calling yourself someone who can build a $10k website. "Mostly sure" isn't the bar. **Actually knowing it** is.

## 🏗️ The build-along

Every module from here builds **one real site** — for a fictional Wollongong builder, **Smith Building Co.** You watch a step happen, then you do the exact same step yourself.

> **The brief, in one box:**
> | | |
> |---|---|
> | **Business** | Smith Building Co — custom home builder |
> | **Location** | Wollongong, services the Illawarra |
> | **Problem** | No website, losing jobs to competitors who look more credible online |
> | **Goal** | A professional site that shows their work and gets the phone ringing |
> | **Has** | Phone photos of past jobs, no logo yet |
> | **Wants** | Full design + build, handled end to end |

Keep this brief in mind. Every module adds one more real piece to this exact site.
